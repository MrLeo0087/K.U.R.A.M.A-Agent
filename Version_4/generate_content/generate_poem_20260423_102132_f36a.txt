Vibrant screen aglow,
Dancing fingers, joyful heart,
Connected, we glow.

In slender hands, it fits,
A world of wonder, at our tips,
Laughter, love, and lights.

With every swipe and tap,
A universe of joy does map,
Endless possibilities.

In this small, shining space,
We find our happy, busy place,
Moments shared with glee.

So let us celebrate,
This tiny marvel, mobile state,
Joy in every byte.
