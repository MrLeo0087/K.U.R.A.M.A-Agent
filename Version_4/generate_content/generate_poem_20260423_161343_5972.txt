Softly falls her smile,
Golden hues upon her face,
Sister, gentle soul.

In laughter, tears, and dreams,
Together we have shared our hearts,
 Memories that gleam.

With every step, she grows,
A woman, strong and free, and wise,
My sister, a rose.

Her eyes, like morning dew,
Reflect the beauty that's within,
A heart that's pure and true.

In her, I see a friend,
A bond that will forever last,
A love that will not end.
