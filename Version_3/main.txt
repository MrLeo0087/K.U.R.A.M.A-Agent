The sky's canvas stretches wide,
A tapestry of blue and gold,
With clouds that drift and reside,
Their shadows dancing, young and old.

The sun's bright brushstrokes paint the day,
With hues of crimson, pink, and grey,
The stars appear, a twinkling sway,
As night's dark veil is lifted away.

The moon, a glowing crescent smile,
Lends gentle light to the sky's dark mile,
The world below, a tiny stage,
Where life's grand drama turns the page.

The sky's vast expanse, a wondrous sea,
Reflects the beauty that's meant to be,
A symbol of hope, freedom, and glee,
A treasure that's forever wild and free.
